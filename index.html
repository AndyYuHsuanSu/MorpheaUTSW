About this resource

Board-certified expertise, distilled

This site was created by a board-certified dermatologist and professor specializing in sclerosing skin diseases at UT Southwestern Medical Center.

The goal is simple: reduce confusion, improve communication between patients and their care teams, and provide a trustworthy starting point for anyone affected by morphea or scleroderma.
